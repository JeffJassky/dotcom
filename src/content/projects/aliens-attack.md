# **Aliens Attack!!\***Writing a physical arcade game in NodeJS\*

### _2016_

We had a team of seven to create an interactive arcade game to promote Fat Cat Fab Lab, a makerspace in the West Village. The game was a hoard of pixel aliens with piezo vibration sensors mounted on servos. A large score board and timer read out the game status. The aliens were shot with a pneumatic ping pong ball gun while a custom made vintage video game soundtrack played.
